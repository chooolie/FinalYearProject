About us

This is a movie recommender application that you can use to view and search through movies here. When you enter in your details you will be able to view movie recommendations for yourself here. You can also create groups or join groups, when you view your group, you will be given movie recommendations for every user in the group.

When viewing movies, you will see a list of movies that users who like that movie also like. You can also choose to rate the movie or to add it to the watchlist of any of your groups. If you then view the group you added your movie too, you can see a list of movies in your watchlist that users have added. You can decide to up or down vote each movie in the group if you like the movie or not. So that next time that you meet up with your group to watch a movie, you can watch the movie with the most votes.

If you want, you can write reviews on movies that you like or dislike here. You can also view other users reviews there too.
